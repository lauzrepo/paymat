## Competitors / Inspiration

### Vertical SaaS (industry-specific)
| Product | Focus | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Jackrabbit | Swim / gymnastics / dance | Very established, complex |
| iClassPro | Swim lessons / gymnastics | Deep feature set, legacy UI |
| Mindbody | Fitness / wellness | Massive but bloated, expensive |
| Pike13 | Fitness studios | Scheduling + billing combined |
| Kicksite | Martial arts | Direct competitor in TKD niche |
| ZenPlanner | Martial arts / fitness | Billing + attendance tracking |

### Horizontal billing / membership tools
| Product | Focus | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| TeamSnap / SportsEngine | Youth sports | Scheduling-first, not billing-first |
| Activity Messenger | Activity businesses | Registration + billing |
| Sawyer | Children's activity programs | Marketplace model |

### Differentiators
- **Multi-tenant from day one** — one platform, many orgs, each with their own portal
- **Stripe Connect** — orgs get paid directly, platform takes a fee
- **Simple by design** — no scheduling or attendance bloat, just billing + member portal
- **White-label per org** — slug-based portal feels native to each business
- **Target gap** — small-to-mid activity businesses that find Mindbody/Jackrabbit overkill
